A Little About Me

I hold a BA in Interdisciplinary English and Communications and am completing an MEd in Instructional Design and Technology at Mount St. Mary's University. My work asks a simple question: what does this learner actually need to understand — and what is the clearest, most purposeful way to give it to them?

Good design, like good prose, makes the invisible visible.
