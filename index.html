FROM THE DESK OF: Kayla Canfield RE: My Approach to Design & Writing

"I believe good design and good writing share the same goal: clarity. Whether I am building a faculty handbook or writing about Flannery O'Connor, I am asking the same question — what does the reader need to understand, and how do I help them get there?"

Background

A designer with a humanities core

Kayla's work sits at the intersection of instructional design and the humanities. She brings literary analysis, cultural research, and archival thinking to the practice of learning design, producing materials that are both pedagogically sound and carefully written.

Her research interests include Southern Gothic fiction, digital humanities, folklore, and the intersection of media and cultural memory.
