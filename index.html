About

The person
behind the work

Kayla Canfield is an Administrative Assistant II at Mount St. Mary's University and a master's student in Instructional Design and Technology. Her work brings together learning design, writing, communication, and systems improvement.

She has developed training materials, organized academic workflows, created communication pieces, and supported projects across English, History, Communications, Political Science, and Sociology. She also continues her work as a writer and researcher with interests in literature, media, culture, American studies, Southern Gothic, digital humanities, and folklore.

Writing & Research

Alongside her design work, Kayla writes about literature, culture, and education. Her academic writing has been recognized by the English Department at Mount St. Mary's University.

Her research interests include Southern Gothic fiction, digital humanities, folklore, and the intersection of media and cultural memory.
